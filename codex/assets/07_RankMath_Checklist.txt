RankMath SEO Protocol Checklist
================================

□ Title ≤60 chars; Meta description ≤150 chars (exact)
□ Slug: short, includes focus KW
□ Focus KW density 0.8–1.3% (never >1.5%) and appears in first 10% of content
□ 8–15 internal links (relevant); 10–15 trusted external citations inside body
□ Affiliate links use rel="sponsored noopener"; add disclosure where needed
□ Short paragraphs (≤50 words); lists/tables for scanability
□ Images with descriptive alt; fixed width/height; lazy loading
□ Add/merge section JSON-LD fragments into page @graph and validate FAQ
□ Mobile performance: pass Lighthouse

End of HS-SEO Codex
